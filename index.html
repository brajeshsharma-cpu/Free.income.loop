REALITY CHECK — READ BEFORE STARTING

This is a real system used by thousands of creators — backed by 2025 creator economy research — but it is not a get-rich-quick scheme. Research confirms faceless channels face a steeper trust-building curve than personal brands, typically needing 3–5 months before algorithms begin amplifying content. Month 1–2 will feel like nothing is working. That's normal. The compounding effect starts around month 4. The single factor that kills this system is quitting before the flywheel starts. Start one niche, one platform, one product. Clone what works. The creator economy rewards consistency and iteration — not perfection.
